about= GeoSIFOR Connector saves and reuses GeoSIFOR/SGIFR service endpoints (WFS, WMS, WMTS, ArcGIS REST, GeoJSON) in QGIS, removing the one-URL-at-a-time cost of adding them through QGIS's standard dialogs every session.
GeoSIFOR is the geospatial information system behind Portugal's SGIFR (Sistema de Gestão Integrada de Fogos Rurais) and coordinated by AGIF. It aggregates spatial data on rural fire from public administration, but every layer behind its viewer is its own service with its own URL and auth setup.
This plugin: paste a URL once, label it, mark it public or restricted, file it into an optional folder, then saved permanently in QGIS settings. One credential, via QGIS's own Authentication Manager, is attached automatically to every restricted entry. A checklist with multi-select, drag-and-drop reordering, favourites, search, and saved profiles loads whichever services you need in one click.

It can use public-only endpoints, or require a GeoSIFOR/PLIS account for restrict-access endpoints. Not an official AGIF/SGIFR tool.
